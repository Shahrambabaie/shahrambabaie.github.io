Shahram Babaie

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the State University of New York at Buffalo. My advisor is Prof. Chunming Qiao.

My research focuses on Quantum Computing, Quantum Error Correction, and Distributed Quantum Computing. I previously earned a Ph.D. in Computer Engineering with a concentration in communication networks and fault-tolerant system design. Motivated by the rapid emergence of quantum technologies as a defining computing paradigm of the 21st century, I began a second Ph.D. to focus deeply on quantum information. My work integrates principles from classical computing, networking, and fault tolerance to develop scalable and reliable quantum systems.
